Humidity

Humidity is not strongly correlated to the latitude of the locations measured, but there are some interesting characteristics displayed. 100% humidity is reached in locations from approximately -50 to +70 degrees latitude but outside of this range, the maximum humidity falls to 80%. Locations that measured 0% humidity were found between 60-80 degrees latitude. Between -20 to 0 degrees latitude, the humidity did not fall below 50%. Perhaps with a larger amount of locations, humidity measurements would show stronger correlation to latitude.
