Cloudiness

Cloudiness is not strongly correlated to the latitude of the locations measured, but there are some interesting characteristics displayed. 100% cloudiness is reached in locations from approximately -20 to +40 degrees latitude with maximum cloudiness falling below 80% below -50 degrees latitude and greater than 70 degrees latitude. 0% cloudiness can be seen throughout the entire range of latitude.
